Our Products

DevBookcase.com

Here at Visoft, we love books, and we know you do as well. We’re hardcore developers who not just work in this field but have a passion for development. Because of this, we have created an application to give back to the developer community and show off our geekiness.

Developer’s Bookcase is an application by developers for developers. Being developers that love to learn, we’ve collected tons of books over the years, in fact too many to count (and sometimes too many to even keep). The goal of Developer’s Bookcase is a place for us developers to hang out, log our books, and discover other books that your fellow geeks own.

Check it out at http://devbookcase.com

EageReader.com

Why should geeks have all the fun? We decided to start a sister site to DevBookcase.com for everyone else.

EageReader is a site for people who love books. People whose physical bookshelves are long past full and who may need to clear out some of these books to make room for other important things, like family members. EageReader allows you to track not only the books you own, but also books you have read, so you can avoid getting home, all excited to crack open your new book only to realize that you have bought this book before. Twice. With EageReader, you will be able to check your book list from anywhere, allowing you to spend less time organizing books and more time reading them.

Check it out at http://eagereader.com

WattTimeIsIt?

No more over/under cooked microwave dinners with WattTimeIsIt? WattTimeIsIt? is an iOS application that converts microwave wattage time differences. Say you have a 1300W microwave, but the dinner your cooking is based on an 1100W one. Just put in the wattages, punch in the time, and you'll get the correct time for your microwave.

ruby_odata

An open-source Ruby consumer for the OData protocol. OData stands for Open Data Protocol. OData is a RESTful data access protocol initially defined by Microsoft. Versions 1.0, 2.0, and 3.0 are released under the Microsoft Open Specification Promise. Version 4.0 is being standardized at OASIS, and was released in March 2014.

Check it out on GitHub or better yet, fork the project!

What Subscription?

Do you often lose track of subscriptions that you have? Whether it's magazines, domain names, VPS hosting, software licenses, etc. there is now a way to keep track of all of those. What Subscription? is the answer. Easy to use and free!

Check it out at https://whatsubscription.com
